5 Unimaginable Things You Can Actually Mindfulness Anywhere

Mindfulness has a conscious meaning at the moment. But have you ever found yourself suddenly lost in your own thoughts? Just so you know this is not a problem. You can overcome this by bringing your attention back to your five senses. That way you can apply mindfulness to any situation. If you can do it while sitting on a chair, why not do it while shopping, chatting with friends, even eating or drinking? Though all of that is an opportunity to apply mindfulness anytime and anywhere. Do you know what the situation is? Well ... here are 5 unimaginable things you actually mindfulness bias anywhere.

Mindfulness When Brushing Your Teeth

What are your habits after waking up guys? Get up, check the smartphone, take a towel and keep showering? Is it always so your waking routine? Well ... from now on try to change the routine for the better. Try it after waking up, pay attention to your feet when resting on the floor. Feel the temperature and texture of your own feet. Then when you brush your teeth, pay attention to the appearance, smell, taste, and texture of your toothpaste. Don't forget to also pay attention to how your legs move from side to side and the sound of brushes on your teeth. Pay attention to the sensation of brush on each tooth and gum.

Mindfulness When Taking Shower

So every time you take a shower, try to be aware and awake, guys Be aware when regulating the temperature of water for bathing, using warm or cold water. Then feel pleasure when warm or cold water is washed in your body. Also be aware of the smell of soap and shampoo you use. Pay attention to the sound and the amount of water you use. Or even just imagine a conversation with your imagination.

Mindfulness When Departing to School or Office

When traveling to school or work, try to become aware of the environment around you. Feel every emotion that comes and comes back inside you. You must be aware of all your senses at that moment. You can imagine being somewhere else, or hope time will quickly leave. It's even fine if you suddenly want to shout loudly or get out of the car. That is one sign if you are aware of yourself.

Mindfulness When Washing Dishes

Not many people know that when washing dishes can be done with mindfulness. So when you are washing dishes, try to be aware when your hands meet water. Feel the difference in water temperature and body temperature. Be aware when you pick up one object at a time. And take 1 to 2 seconds to clean it thoroughly. And sometimes this gives rise to thoughts that come and come back. In fact, sometimes this makes you aware if you want to do something. And in the end you feel satisfied after you finish washing the dishes.

Mindfulness While Waiting at Queue

Waiting for the queue often feels tiring and boring. Do you know why? Because your mind is too focused on the long queues. So next time if you are waiting in line, try to be aware of your reaction when you first see the queue line. Then pay attention to your posture while standing waiting there. But is that enough? Well ... try to watch the breath when you focus on physical sensations in your body. Watch how you react each time you step forward in the queue. You have to be aware when you always look at your watch or just check your cellphone, just when you are looking for some kind of distraction. And in the end remember your interactions with customer service when you are served.
